#cfgmgmtcamp

1 and 2 February 2016

Gent, Belgium

Puppet architecture for 2000 nodes using AWS infrastructure

Alexandru Loredan Stancu

Puppet Track - Tuesday, 2/2/2016 14:00 - B1.015

"Creating an Puppet architecture for 2000 nodes in AWS can be challenging and can lead to unwanted complexity if it's not designed in the right way. Presentation will show how to create such an architecture and will include puppet setup, hiera design, dynamic facts but also roles and profile design pattern."

About Alexandru Loredan Stancu

Works as an TechOps Engineer; - 5 years experience with Puppet; - Puppet Certified Professional with experience in managing big infrastructures on AWS cloud; - The organizer of Puppet User Group in Bucharest; - LPIC certified with 8 years Linux Sys Admin experience.
