FOSDEM Configuration Management Devroom

The weekend before CfgMgmtCamp.eu the famous FOSDEM conference takes place in Brussels. At FOSDEM, there will be a devroom dedicated to configuration management. The Configuration Management Devroom is taking place on Saturday, February 1.

We are looking for talks from real world case studies, brainstorming and hacking around configuration and systems management from complex virtual environments and cloud computing to desktops and laptops. There is huge interest in automation, monitoring, performance management, configuration management, and DevOps. We are looking for presentations from people who are working in the field on these topics:

  • Configuration and Systems Management theory & principles
  • Configuration and Systems Management tools - with an emphasis on real world use cases
  • Techniques and case studies
  • Audit, Compliance and Security
  • Various DevOps topics, including collaboration and training tips.

Devroom Deadlines

Since the Configuration Management Devroom is held at the FOSDEM conference, it also follows the date guideline of FOSDEM. Here's an overview of the most important dates:

  • 1/12/2013 : Devroom CFP Ends
  • 13/12/2013 : Speakers notified
  • 30/12/2013 : Agenda published on FOSDEM site
  • 1/2/2014 : Configuration Management Devroom starts

Submit a FOSDEM Devroom Talk

They are asking everyone to use Pentabarf for submissions this year. Here's what you need to do to submit a talk to the Configuration Management Devroom:

  • Go to the FOSDEM submission page.
  • Select FOSDEM 2014.
  • Follow the instructions to submit a talk.
  • Select "Configuration management devroom" as the "track".
  • Propose your session.

If you have any questions about the devroom, feel free to hop onto #cfgmgmtcamp on freenode IRC. As a last option you can email the DevRoom organisers Dawn Foster or Walter Heck directly, but to discourage that you'll have to hunt down their direct email addresses elsewhere.
