Gent, Belgium

Building an IP-EVPN fabric with Cumulus Linux

Attilla de Groot - Tuesday , 7/2/2017, 14:40 - 15:20 - B1.017

Cumulus Linux is a NOS that runs on 50+ different hardware platforms used in datacenters. EVPN is a new RFC that allows to build a L2 overlay network with BGP as the control plane. This makes it possible to offer "classic" L2 services on a scalable IP fabric. Cumulus Linux can be natively configured using Ansible or any other open-source orchestration tool.

About Speaker

Attilla has spent the last 15 years at the cutting edge of networking, having spent time with KPN, Amsterdam Internet Exchange and HP, with exposure to technology from Cis- co, HP, Juniper and Huawei. He now works for Cumulus Networks, the creators of open networking, where he is able to continue his interest in open architecture design.
