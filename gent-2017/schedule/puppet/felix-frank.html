#cfgmgmtcamp

6 and 7 February 2017

Gent, Belgium

Extending Puppet within reason and beyond

Felix Frank - Monday, Feb 6, 14:00 - 14:40 - B1.015

Puppet is a de facto standard for managing infrastructure of varying complexity. Not only does it come with a powerful standard tool chain, it also includes countless extension points and APIs.

This presentation highlights some of the useful ways in which Puppet can be customized and extended. It collects some of the outrageous stunts the community has pulled off on their basis.

Puppet and mgmt- better together

Felix Frank - Tuesday, Feb 7, 15:40 - 16:20 - B4.042

About Speaker

Felix is an old school Puppet user and serial contributor. His history includes tons of mailing list posts, upstream patches as well as modules and help with the Vox Pupuli effort.

More recently, he also went deep into using Ansible and started contributing, and was among the first contributors to mgmt.

Felix is a systems architect for The unbelievable Machine Company.
