#cfgmgmtcamp

6 and 7 February 2017

Gent, Belgium

Getting data to the end user quickly

Tom Barber - Tuesday, February 7 , 12:20 - 13:00 - D.AUD

Tom's day job involves getting large volumes of data to end users often in different parts of the world. Find out how we leverage systems automation and a range of services to get data to our end users in the most efficient way possible. Combining the stability and extendability of Puppet, and the scalability and flexibility of Juju we ensure our servers globally are put to work in the most efficient way possible both when deploying services to bare metal or to containers.

About Speaker

Tom Barber is a Developer and Systems guy currently working for NASA JPL's Data Applications office. He helps design and develop a wide range of applications from Genomics search platforms to Dark Web Crawlers looking for criminals.
