#cfgmgmtcamp

2 and 3 February 2015

Gent, Belgium

SaltStack Community Room (B.3.039)

SaltStack builds fast and scalable systems management software that also happens to include an extremely flexible configuration management engine. SaltStack is also used for any cloud orchestration, data center automation and server provisioning. SaltStack is unique in providing Web-scale orchestration for complex IT infrastructure using event-driven, predictive automation to control physical and virtual infrastructure and application environments.

SaltStack was founded in August 2012 to support the Salt project and to deliver SaltStack Enterprise packaged for the world's most-dynamic large and small businesses, and cloud infrastructures. Today, Salt is one of the biggest, most-active open source projects in the world, and is proud to have more than 1300 active developers engaged and contributing every day to make better systems and confiuration managemen software.

Join Thomas Hatch, SaltStack CTO and co-founder, and Dave Boucha, SaltStack senior engineer, in the SaltStack community room for a tutorial, talks, networking, coding and open discussion.
