About ConfigManagementCamp

ConfigManagementCamp is the event for people interested in open source configuration management to meet each other. ConfigManagementCamp will be the event where different communities will meet and exchange ideas both internally and within the broader community.

Community members are users and/or contributors. The broader community can and will be anyone else who is interested in participating in this event, such as industry consultants, integrators, analysts, commentators, bloggers, journalists and so on.

Taking place right after FOSDEM in Brussels, Belgium, you can do 2 open source events in 1 trip, so more value for your money. Taking place in Gent (a 30 minute train ride from Brussels), you can visit 2 Belgian cities, rather than one, and fall in love with Gent.

ConfigManagementCamp will have different rooms for each community, hackspaces, trainings, workshops and a general track with keynotes all about Configuration Management topics. We've created separate pages for some of the community rooms to make it easier to find topics you are interested in for a particular community: Chef, Puppet, Rudder and CFEngine, and more coming soon.

Location

CfgMgmtCamp 2014 will take place at the SchoonMeersen Campus of the University College Gent. This campus is conveniently located at walking distance from the central train station in Gent and 2 minutes from the E40 Highway from Gent to Brussels. Note that there are several university campus locations in Gent, so please make sure that you are going to the one just south of the Gent-Sint-Pieters railway station. If you type the location into Google, it will most likely take you to the incorrect campus, but if you type "BIB SchoonMeersen", you should end up in the right place.
