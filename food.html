Food

Lunch

Lunch is available in the campus cafeteria / catine at own expense in the 'Resto D' opposite the D auditorium.
If you are a speaker, organizer, sponsor, please find an organizer for your food voucher.

The main menu for each day are listed here, there is also a soup of the day and a desert.
There is a western vegaterian, an eastern vegaterian, a vegan, and a gluten-free option, please inquire with the cafeteria staff.

A meal costs 7,70 EUR, there is an assortment of desserts ranging from 1,40 EUR to 2,50 EUR.
Please note that all prices advertised in the cafeteria are the only correct prices.
The cafeteria accepts vouchers, cash money and bank cards, such as Bancontact/Mister Cash, Maestro, MasterCard and VISA.
