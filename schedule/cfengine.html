#Cfgmgmtcamp

6 and 7 February 2017

Gent, Belgium

CFEngine Community Room

CFEngine is small, fast, secure, and flexible automation tool. It is designed to check compliance of the desired state continually with minimal overhead.

A few things that make CFEngine stand out:

  • Battle tested over time. Originally released in 1993 and has been through two major re-factors.
  • Written in C, CFEngine is very portable and can run on almost any kind of device. You can find CFEngine inside typical datacenter servers, crypto currency banking nodes, transit systems, and even at the core of . This portability allows management from the smallest of your devices to the largest using a common language.
  • CFEngine is flexible allowing you to specify with granular detail how a system should look in different environments and weather or not it should be automatically repaired.

CFEngine is an established project with many 10s of thousands of managed nodes reported. It is most commonly used in large complex environments that have compliance auditing needs and who value small and lightweight tools.

Join us in the CFEngine Community Room for some presentations and open discussions.
