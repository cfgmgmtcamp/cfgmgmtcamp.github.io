#cfgmgmtcamp

6 and 7 February 2017

Gent, Belgium

CFEngine simplified with EFL

Neil Watson - Tuesday, February 7, 15:40 - 16:25 Time - B3.036

The Evolve Free Library, EFL, is an add on policy library for CFEngine. Its data driven, low level design makes CFEngine simpler to use and maintain.

About Speaker

Neil has been practicing system administration and devops for over 20 years in the spheres of manufacturing, software development, service provider, finance, and professional services. He has been using CFEngine for 10 years and was a CFEngine 3 early adopter. Neil wrote the original CFEngine cookbook, and the CFEngine 3 tools EFL, Delta Reporting, #cfengine's cfbot, and the VIM plugin.
