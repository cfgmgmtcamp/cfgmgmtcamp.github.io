Gent, Belgium

CFEngine Kick Off

Christian Linden - Monday, February 6, 15:40 - 16:20 - B3.036

CFEngine is easy to use if one knows how. The way to there might be painful. It took me a year until being able to get promises running and doing on the nodes what I wanted them to do. With an expert at my side this could have been shortened to a months maybe. This is what I provide: help in getting up and running with CFEngine what makes system administration much easier and controllable. If you touch a system by hand, except the CFEngine hub (the policy server), you might consider doing something wrong.
