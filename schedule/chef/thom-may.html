InSpec Tips & Tricks

Thom May

InSpec Tips & Tricks

Tuesday, February 6, 14:40

InSpec is an open-source testing framework for infrastructure with a human-readable language for specifying compliance, security and other policy requirements. This talk will provide an introduction to InSpec and then look at some tips and tricks that can provide supowers to this simple framework.

Some of the tips and tricks included are:

  • inspec shell
  • Wrapper Profiles
  • Using Test Kitchen for integration testing

About Thom May

Thom has been operating computers professionally for over half his life. In that time he's learned that he much prefers being able to test that they do the correct thing. He's currently lucky enough to do full time open source development for a living, as Community Engineering Lead at Chef.
